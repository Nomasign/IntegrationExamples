# Creating a NomaSign Account

You need a NomaSign account on a plan that includes the **Integration** feature. If you already have one, skip to [verifying your plan](#already-have-an-account) below.

## How NomaSign accounts work

- When you create a NomaSign account, you are automatically the **owner** of your organization.
- The **Integration** feature is a plan-level permission — it applies to the entire organization.
- As the org owner, you control billing, invitations, and feature access.

## Steps

1. Go to [app.nomasign.com](https://app.nomasign.com)
2. Click **Sign Up** and create your account (or log in if you already have one)
3. Subscribe to a plan that includes **Integration**

## Already have an account?

If you already have a NomaSign account, verify your plan includes the Integration feature:

1. Log in at [app.nomasign.com](https://app.nomasign.com) as the **org owner**
2. Go to **Account Settings** → **Subscription**
3. Confirm that **Integration** is listed as an included feature on your plan

> **Don't see "Integration" on your plan?** You'll need to upgrade. Go to **Account Settings** → **Subscription** → **Upgrade**, or contact [support@nomasign.com](mailto:support@nomasign.com) if you need help choosing a plan.

> **Not the org owner?** Only the org owner can view or change the subscription. Ask your organization's account owner to verify integration access or upgrade the plan.

## Testing & sandbox

NomaSign does not have a separate sandbox environment. Integration features work in production — but you can test freely by sending documents to your own email addresses. API calls on your plan are not billed per-call; they're included with the Integration feature.

Once your plan includes Integration, you're ready to invite a dedicated integrator account.

> **Stuck?** See the [FAQ](./faq.md) or [Troubleshooting](./troubleshooting.md) for common issues with account setup and plans.
