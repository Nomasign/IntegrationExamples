# Step 2 — Troubleshooting

Common problems when inviting or setting up the integrator account.

---

| Problem | Cause | Solution |
|---------|-------|----------|
| **Integrator** role not in dropdown | Plan doesn't include Integration | Check Step 1 — verify or upgrade your plan |
| Invite email doesn't arrive | Spam filter or typo | Check spam/junk, verify the email address, re-send from the Manage Organization page |
| Got signed out of owner account | Same browser session | You logged in as integrator in the same browser — use a separate browser or incognito window |
| Email is already in another org | Account conflict | The email must not already belong to a different NomaSign organization. Use a different email or have it removed from the other org first |
| Invite shows "pending" indefinitely | Invitation not accepted | The integrator must open the email and complete signup. Re-send if the email was lost |
| Integrator can't see other users' templates | Expected behavior | Templates are user-scoped — the integrator only sees their own. This is by design (see Step 3) |
