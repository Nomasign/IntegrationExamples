# Creating a NomaSign Account

This is just a regular NomaSign account — nothing special. If you already have one, skip to verifying your plan below.

## Steps

1. Go to [app.nomasign.com](https://app.nomasign.com)
2. Click **Sign Up** and create your account (or log in if you already have one)
3. Ensure your plan includes **integration access**

## Already have an account?

If you already have a NomaSign account, you just need to verify that your plan supports integrations:

1. Log in at [app.nomasign.com](https://app.nomasign.com)
2. Go to your **Account Settings** → **Subscription**
3. Confirm your plan includes integration/API access
4. If not, upgrade to a plan that includes it

> **No integration option visible?** Your current plan doesn't support integrations. You'll need to upgrade before proceeding.

Once your account is active with integration access, you can invite an integrator and start building.
