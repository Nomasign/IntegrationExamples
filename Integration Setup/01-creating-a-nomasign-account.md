# Creating a NomaSign Account

This is just a regular NomaSign account — nothing special. If you already have one, this step is done and you can skip to the next guide.

## Steps

1. Go to [app.nomasign.com](https://app.nomasign.com)
2. Click **Sign Up** and create your account
3. Subscribe to a plan that includes integration access

Once your account is active, you can invite an integrator and start building.
