# Step 2 — Creating an Integration Account

Invite a dedicated email address as an **Integrator** in your organization. This account gets API access automatically when assigned the Integrator role.

This will be an account like `sign@yourdomain.com` — this is what signers will see as the sender after you've sent a document via the API.

> **Important:** The Integrator role is what grants API access. Make sure you select **Integrator** in the invite dialog — regular Member/Admin roles have integrator access but not for generating tokens.

## Who performs this step?

You must be logged in as the **org owner**. Only org owners can invite members and assign the Integrator role.

## Steps

### 1. Click on the Profile button

Log in at [app.nomasign.com](https://app.nomasign.com) as the org owner and click your profile image in the top-right corner.

![Click profile button](./images/step-1.png)

### 2. Invite & Manage Teams

Select **Invite & Manage Teams** from the profile menu.

![Invite and Manage Teams menu item](./images/step-2.png)

### 3. Invite Member

On the **Manage Organization** tab, click **Invite Member**.

![Invite Member button](./images/step-3.png)

### 4. Invite as Integrator

Enter your integrator email address (e.g. `signing@yourdomain.com`), set the role to **Integrator**, and send the invite.

![Invite dialog with Integrator role selected](./images/step-4.png)

### 5. Accept & Log In as Integrator

Accept the invitation email, then log in with the integrator account.

> **Browser tip:** Accept the invite in a **different browser** or **incognito window**. If you're already logged in as the org owner, the integrator login will sign you out.

You'll see the **Integration** page after logging in.

![Integration page](./images/step-5.png)
